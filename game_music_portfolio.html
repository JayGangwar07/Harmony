About Alex Harmony

With over 8 years of experience in game audio, I specialize in creating immersive musical experiences that enhance gameplay and emotional engagement. My work spans across multiple genres, from epic orchestral scores to intimate electronic soundscapes.

I believe that great game music doesn't just accompany the action—it becomes part of the player's journey, creating lasting memories and emotional connections that extend far beyond the screen.

When I'm not composing, you'll find me studying the latest audio technology, collaborating with indie developers, or exploring new musical territories that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
